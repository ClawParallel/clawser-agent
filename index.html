What is CLAWSER?

CLAWSER is a fully autonomous AI agent deployed onchain to hunt, analyze, and execute trades across multiple EVM-compatible networks — without human intervention.

Powered by a proprietary neural signal engine, CLAWSER continuously monitors mempool activity, liquidity shifts, wallet clustering, and social velocity to identify high-probability targets before they move.

When a target is locked, CLAWSER executes with sub-20ms precision — entering, managing, and exiting positions based on real-time onchain intelligence. Every hunt is logged immutably to the chain.

// 04 — ARCHITECTURE

How CLAWSER Hunts

01
SCAN
CLAWSER's surveillance layer monitors mempool, DEX pools, wallet clusters, and social sentiment in real-time. Every signal is scored and ranked by confidence.
02
TARGET
The neural signal engine cross-references multi-dimensional data to lock high-probability targets. False positives are filtered by adversarial rejection layers.
03
EXECUTE
Atomic multi-hop transactions are constructed and fired directly to validators. Entry, sizing, and slippage are calculated autonomously in-flight.
04
MANAGE
Active positions are monitored every block. Dynamic stop-loss adjusts to volatility. Partial takes are executed when momentum signals degrade.
05
EXIT
Exit triggers fire when price targets, time windows, or reversal patterns are detected. All exits are gas-optimized for maximum net return.
06
LEARN
Every hunt — win or loss — feeds back into the training pipeline. CLAWSER continuously improves signal accuracy from live market data.
